    Who are We

    WASCA is West African Society for Communication and Administration. It is an independent non-political, and non-governmental but professional organization with the desire of integrating the vast members of the sub-region irrespective of their discipline into a socially and economically vibrant community through effective communication. West African Society for Communication and Administration (WASCA) is recognized by law under CAMA Act of 1990 with Registration Number 55681.
